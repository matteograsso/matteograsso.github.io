## IIT Wiki — [iit.wiki](https://www.iit.wiki)

A community reference for Integrated Information Theory: its postulates,
formalism, and key results — written to be accessible to newcomers and precise
enough for researchers.

## IIT intrinsic ontology — *Wiki Part 3*

An in-depth treatment of the intrinsic ontology of IIT: what exists from the
intrinsic perspective of a system, and how cause–effect power grounds the
existence and structure of experience.

## Active research themes

- **Temporal experience** — *why time feels the way it does*: a principled,
  structure-based account of the felt flow and form of time.
- **Phenomenal objects** — how IIT accounts for the objects of experience.
- **Qualia Structure** — relating the structure of experience to the
  cause–effect structure of its substrate.
