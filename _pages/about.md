I'm a Research Scientist in the Department of Psychiatry at the
**University of Wisconsin–Madison**, working on the science of consciousness.
My research develops **Integrated Information Theory (IIT)** — both its formal
framework and its philosophical foundations — to explain how the quality and
structure of experience arise from the cause–effect power of a physical
substrate.

I hold a Ph.D. in Philosophy from Roma Tre University, and my work spans
computational neuroscience, philosophy of mind, and metaphysics.

## Research interests

- Integrated Information Theory: formalism and foundations
- The phenomenal structure of experience — space, time, and objects
- Causation and causal structure; causal reductionism
- Philosophy of mind, metaphysics, and the mind–body problem
